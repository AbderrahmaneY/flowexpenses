## âœ… Approving Expenses

### Step 1: View Pending Approvals
From your Dashboard or click **Approvals** in the nav menu.

### Step 2: Review Each Expense
For each pending expense, you can see:
- Employee name
- Expense title and amount
- Category and date
- Attached receipts (click "View" for details)

### Step 3: Take Action

| Action | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Approve** | Expense is valid and complete |
| **Reject** | Expense is invalid or not allowed |
| **Request Details** | Need more info (receipt, explanation) |

### Approving
Click the green **Approve** button.

### Rejecting
1. Click the red **Reject** button
2. Enter a reason (required)
3. Confirm

### Requesting Details
1. Click **Details** button
2. Write what information you need
3. The employee will be notified to re-submit
