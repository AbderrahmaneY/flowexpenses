## â• Creating a New Expense

### Step 1: Click "New Expense"
The button is in the top right of your dashboard.

### Step 2: Fill in the Details

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Title** | Brief description | "Client Lunch" |
| **Category** | Type of expense | Meal, Transport, Hotel |
| **Date** | When the expense occurred | 12/12/2025 |
| **Amount** | Cost in your currency | 45.00 |
| **Currency** | USD, EUR, MAD, GBP | USD |

### Step 3: Upload Receipt (Optional)
- Click "Upload files"
- Select your receipt image or PDF
- Maximum file size: 2MB

### Step 4: Submit
- Click **Submit Expense** to send for approval
- Or click **Save Draft** to finish later
